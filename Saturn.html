Sound of Saturn

The crossover of Saturn and vinyl.

Sound of Saturn

The idea was inspired while I was trying to model a vinyl record. During the process, the shape of vinyl kinda reminded me of Saturn. Therefore, an idea came into my mind. Merging the elements of vinyl and Saturn with one another.

The 3D models

The hardest part of this project is trying to get the planet scene. The planet was made by displacement effect. Besides, the lighting is also very important in order to achieve the scene I wanted.

Planet Scene

Fun fact that after I posted it on Instagram. A friend reminded me that Saturn is actually a gas planet, not a rocky planet. LoL, I should have found out earlier.
