Ferrari F1 Steering Wheel

I recreated a Formula 1 Steering Wheel in Cinema 4D

Motive

The Formula 1 2021 season was absolutely fascinating. And I become a big fan of formula 1 since I watched Netflix F1 Documentary [Drive to Survive] in 2020. Although some rivalries of drivers in the show is exaggerate, it is the best way to let a newbie start to know more about Formula 1.

And because I am now a big fan of Formula 1. I always wanted to create something related to Formula 1. I tried modelling a formula 1 car, but it is too hard for me right now. It was so so hard to get every details right since F1 cars are very complicated machines. Therefore, I tried to model other things instead, a Ferrari F1 Steering Wheel. This is a 3D F1 steering wheel model I recreated on Cinema 4D.

The Model

The main reason I choose this particular steering wheel to recreate is because there are lots of references on the internet. So that I can find a lot of pictures as a reference to model. I think the base of the steering wheel is the hardest part because I have to get the shape right. Fortunately the base is symmetry, so I just have to model half of the base and mirror the other half. Once I got the base done, whatâ€™s left was just the bottoms and their labels. Pure Grinding! It was not hard though, but it took me a couple days to finish it all. When everything was done and with proper lighting, the result is quite stunning.

The Alternatives

I added a Ferrari text logo in the middle of the steering wheel. The original one does not have the logo perhaps they wanted to reduce weight. But I like to add a text logo on it and I think it fits in quite well.
