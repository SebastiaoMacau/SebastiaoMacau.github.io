Wine

GLASS AND WINE BOTTLE PRACTICE

The Idea

The reference of this practice is a wine I bought from Marks & Spencer. And I think it's made by a wine brand called "OPORTO". I love the design of the bottle, really stand out among the wines. Thus, tried to replicate one in Cinema4D as my practice.


The Model and Material

To be honest, it's not hard to replicate the shape of the bottle as I've found a lot of reference picture on the internet. The liquid's texture is a little bit hard to make, tried a couple few times to get it right. I added a glass next to it in order to make the sence less boring.


The Lighting

I think the lighting is the hardest part of this work. The reflextion of the glass is kinda hard to control. I tried to keep the reflextion as clean as possible and make sure there is enough light to light up the sence. It's not easily, I keep adjusting the position and angle of the lights and white planes. Keep learning from mistakes. And at the end, the result looking OK. Should have do more lighting practice. LOL
