馬歇爾

Changing the iconic Marshall Logo into Chinese.

Marshall

"馬歇爾" is the Chinese translation of Marshall. I am making Marshall's Stanmore II Speaker as my practice. Instead of 100% recreated the speaker, I wanteed to make a little alteration. Therefore, replaced the iconic "Marshall" logo into a Chinese one. In the end, looking like a mixture of Western rock 'n' roll culture and Chinese culture. 你懂唔懂腰滾架？ LMAO

The 3D models

The model is quite simple, to be honest. It's just a box with buttons on it. Whereas the hardest part of this project is trying to get the texture right. Expecially the leather material.
