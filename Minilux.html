Coca-Cola Camera

What if... Coca-cola was a camera brand, what would their camera look like?

The Coca-Cola Minilux

As many people may find that Coca-Cola's logo and Leica's logo looks alike. Well, at least I do feel they look alike. Perhaps, if Coca-Cola manufacture camera, it would be like Leica's. Therefore, I created a camera model based on Leica's Minilux. Instead of paste a Leica's logo on it, I switch switch it to a Coca-Cola's logo. I think it would be cool if they did a crossover. LoL.

The 3D models

This is the 3D models I created for this project. The camera's body is the hardest part because each edge's arch is different. It's more than just a regular box and it was hard to get it right. Once the body was done, the len is just combination of the cylinders.
