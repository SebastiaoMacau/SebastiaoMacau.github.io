BEHANCE

Recently, I joined Behance community. I had heard about this community of designer before, but I did not set up an account until last month. I found that Behance really gather a lot of outstanding designers and it also offer a great space to display your artworks. In Behance, user can upload their works as a project and add descriptions to explain it. Which is the ideal place for people to display their works. Wish I had found out earlier, then I will not have to build my own website. Nevertheless, building a website by code is a good experience and it is quite fun, to be honest. YOUTUBE

Apart from more practices in Cinema4D. I also started to develop my second interest - SimRacing. I made some videos on YouTube, recording myself playing racing games. At first, I tried to make some short videos around 3~5 mins. Maybe doing some hot-laps. And then, I have also tried to record some long videos. Recording the whole race and commenting it. It show huge difference between short and long videos. Well, obviously people are too busy to watch long videos. (Or maybe my videos are too boring, in fact, they are. LoL) For long videos, I just got a dozen views. And for short videos, it can reach hundreds. And for now, my channel only got 3 subscribers. LoL. I do not know who they are, but I really appreciate them.
